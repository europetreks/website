OUR TEAM

TREVOR & KATHERINE LEE

Trevor Lee has a wide range of experience in traveling alone and in groups. He participated in study abroad programs while in school and has traveled to over 35 countries on 5 continents. His travels incorporated leisure, schooling and mission trips that included living abroad for over two years. He is a graduate from the University of Texas at Austin where he received degrees in History & Religious Studies, along with over three years of Bible School here in the U.S. and Australia. He and Katie married in 2011 and recently welcomed their second daughter into the adventure family. They currently live in Munich, Germany.

Katie Lee has traveled to nearly 30 countries on 5 continents. She graduated from Saint Edwards University here in Austin, Texas and received a degree in Early Childhood Education. Before attending Bible School for two years, she taught preschool and gymnastics. She and her husband served the Lord on the University of Texas campus for over 5 years.
