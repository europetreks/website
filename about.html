ABOUT

NOT ALL WHO WANDER ARE LOST

Europe Treks is for the dreamers. It is for people who love scratch-off maps and wander around the city to see what adventures they can run into.

We will be exploring the sites of John Wycliffe, John Huss, Martin Luther, William Tyndale, Count Zinzendorf, and many more to see the significance of church history to our current Christian life and how much religion is tied to history and shaped our lives today.

OUR VISION

Traveling internationally will provide an excellent opportunity to experience different cultures and increase awareness of world events. As staff, we have found through our own experiences that traveling abroad expands our views and develops our level of maturity, making us more well-rounded, virtuous members of society. Our trips contain curriculum that is designed to give each participant a clearer idea of sites they will be visiting throughout their trek. These treks are designed by travelers for the participants to experience a “mini study abroad program” in addition to traveling and seeing other cultures that will broaden their view of the world we live in today.
